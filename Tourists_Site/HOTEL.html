The Golden Crown Hotel - Kandy

The Golden Crown Hotel is a 5-star hotel located in Ampitya, Kandy, Srilanka. Set amidst lush paddy fields surrounded by lush greens, the property is just 10-minute drive away from the famous Temple of the Tooth Relic.Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and comes with a flat-screen satellite TV. Some rooms have a seating area for your convenience. You will find a kettle and minibar in the room. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bidet and shower, with bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries provided.

Riff Hikkaduwa - Galle

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Located in Hikkaduwa, 1.1 km from Hikkaduwa Beach, Riff Hikkaduwa provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. Each accommodation at the 5-star hotel has pool views, and guests can enjoy access to a bar and to a garden. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.All units are equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a kettle, a bidet, a hairdryer and a desk. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries, rooms at the hotel also offer a sea view. At Riff Hikkaduwa each room includes a seating area.

JKAB Beach Resort - Trinco

This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Situated in Trincomalee, a few steps from Uppuveli beach, JKAB Beach Resort features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a shared lounge. Featuring a garden, the property is located within 4.1 km of Fort Frederick. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.All rooms are fitted with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a fridge, a kettle, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. The resort offers some rooms that have a balcony and pool view, and the rooms have a private bathroom and a wardrobe. The rooms at JKAB Beach Resort are equipped with a seating area.
